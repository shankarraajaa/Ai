AI Tool-Building Challenge Submission
Tool Name: AI Customer 360 Dashboard
1. Short Explanation of the Problem Solved
Customer information is often spread across different systems like CRM, support tickets, and emails. This makes it difficult for sales and customer success teams to quickly understand a customer's situation.
The AI Customer 360 Dashboard combines data from these sources into one view. It summarizes customer information, highlights risks and opportunities, and suggests the next best action to help teams make faster decisions.
2. Tool / Workflow
Technology Used:
Python,Streamlit,Pandas
Workflow:
Load CRM, Support, and Email data.
Match records using Customer ID.
Display customer details in one dashboard.
Generate customer summary, risks, opportunities, health score, and next best action.

4. Dummy Data Used 
The project uses three sample CSV files:
CRM Data: Company details, deal stage, deal value, renewal date.
Support Data: Ticket issues, priority, and status.
Email Data: Latest customer communication.
These datasets are connected using Customer ID.
5. Logic Used
The dashboard combines data from all three sources and applies simple business rules.
It checks for:
Open support tickets
Negative customer feedback
Critical product issues
Based on this, it generates:
Customer Summary
Risks
Opportunities
Customer Health Score
Recommended Next Best Action
6. Example Input and Output
Example Input
Customer: Acme Corp
Stage: Negotiation
Deal Value: $25,000
Open Ticket: Login Issue
Email: "We are unsure about renewing because response times have been slow."
Example Output
Summary: Customer is in the negotiation stage with an upcoming renewal.
Risks:
Open support ticket
Customer unhappy with response time
Opportunity:
High-value deal with good closing potential
Next Best Action:
 Resolve the support issue and schedule a renewal discussion.
7. Future Improvements
With more time, I would connect the dashboard to real platforms like Zoho CRM, Gmail, and Slack using APIs. I would also add AI-based sentiment analysis, churn prediction, and real-time customer insights.
